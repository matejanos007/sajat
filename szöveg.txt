About Mitsubishi Motors
Mitsubishi Motors Corporation (三菱自動車工業株式会社, Mitsubishi Jidōsha Kōgyō KK) is a public company traded on the Tokyo Stock Exchange (TYO: 7211). Its headquarters is located in the Shibaura district of Minato, Tokyo, Japan.

History
Founded on April 22, 1970, Mitsubishi Motors is a spin-off from the automobile division of Mitsubishi Heavy Industries. Over the years, the company has grown to become a significant player in the global automotive industry.
